Allotment Adventure

Simple, "open-garden" game where the player can do whatever they wish. There are no set objectives, but all player interactions in the garden are tracked and saved in a JSON file. Feel free to have a play - see what objects you can pick up and feel free to talk to the NPC to get some cute quests.

The Bioverse Project

The Bioverse is a metaverse and web3 social impact network targeting those already engaged with, or wanting to engage with, Climate Crisis action. The creation of a metaverse network is a once in a generation opportunity to redesign our world with inclusivity, diversity, accountability and love for our planet as foundational principles.

Please feel free to look around our Phase 1 Bioverse prototype.

Danu - AR Filter

Current WIP.

Using BlendShapes and Unity, we are creating an AR filter for the Danu character, the main face of the Bioverse. We will use Danu as a cross-platform ambassador for the project, connecting the real world with the virtual.
