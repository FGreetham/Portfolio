The Bioverse

Our first phase Bioverse prototype is now ready to explore. Please take a look at the space on itch below and also check out our website for more project information.

Enter the Bioverse

The Concept

The Bioverse is a metaverse and web3 social impact network targeting those wanting to engage with Climate Crisis action. The creation of a metaverse network is a once in a generation opportunity to redesign our digital world with inclusivity, diversity, accountability and love for our planet as foundational principles.

Aiming to connect people back to nature through technology, and created with Biophilic and Nudge design principles, this is a space for education, play and healing within the digital realm.

Development So Far

Our team of 4 have been creating the phase 1 prototype of the Bioverse using Unity. I am the Lead Developer on this project, working alongside a creative director, a 3D generalist and a sound designer.

I have been using C# to create various functions within the 3D space and we have been using SourceTree for version control.

Team Work and Version Control

The team used SourceTree to share and merge our project files. As the Lead developer, I was the main point of contact for the rest of the team with regards to setting up their SourceTree files, linking their Personal Access Tokens and troubleshooting any issues.

I enjoyed the challenges that came from working with the team completely remotely, for instance quickly troubleshooting when people had errors merging files, when someone deleted all their assets and needed to revert the project to an earlier version and advising on deployment.

Animating Characters

Both the Player and NPC are animated using Mixamo animations, including walking, side stepping and waving. When the player moves backwards the walking animation is reversed to differentiate between forwards and backwards. When the player looks in the mirror they can see themselves side stepping. Also if the player walks up to the NPC Danu in the scene, they will stop following their pathway, turn to the player and wave. They will then wait until the player walks away before returning to walking their path.

NPC Pathway

I set up the pathway for the NPC to walk using NavMesh and an array of Transforms. Using Random Range the NPC is then set to move between the different Transform points as the compiler wishes. This was an upgrade on using defined Vector3 positions in an array in the code, as now other members of the team can add or change Transforms as they wish.

Interactive Space

Wanting to keep the biophillic design of the space, we created round a round mesh to play videos which were provided by the Guardians Worldwide project whose aim is to facilitate education on indigenous communities globally.

To create the mirror, I used the input of a second camera as a render texture and flipped it to reflect the scene.
